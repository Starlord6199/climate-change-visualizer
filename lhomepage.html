Keep It Cool!

Given the extreme effects and consequences of climate change in the short and long-term, coupled with the lack of awareness in general public, 'Keep It Cool!' would serve as a novel visualization tool to display the harsh effects of climate change in real-time in an intitutive manner. We also aim to track down the effects of some factors like CO2(Carbon Dioxide) on climate change and provide useful insights/feedback to each user.
